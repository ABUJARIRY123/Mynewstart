It is a program specifically for students of knowledge, with four semesters of classes spanning two years. The Goal of the Program: That the student graduates having studied a variety of Shari’ah sciences, and having gone through their various chapters, as this will be a key for him to build his path for further studies in the Shari’ah sciences. The first semester begins in September of each year, and ends in mid-December.
